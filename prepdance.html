Cornish College of the Arts Preparatory Dance redesign department redesign

In 2018 I worked on the redsign of the Preparatory Dance redesign. The site was launched in summer of 2018.

Visual Redesign

The purpose of the redesign was to simplfy the site and add more media. Images for every class was added and I added the abilty to filter classes by discipline and age. I also intergrated an events page that the Preparatory admin could add, update or delete.
